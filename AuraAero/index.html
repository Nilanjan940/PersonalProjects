About Aura Aero

Redefining urban mobility for a sustainable future

Aura Aero was founded in 2035 with a vision to transform urban transportation through sustainable aviation technology. Our mission is to reduce urban congestion and carbon emissions by providing safe, efficient, and accessible air mobility solutions.

With headquarters in San Francisco and operations in major metropolitan areas worldwide, we're leading the transition to eco-friendly urban air travel. Our team of engineers, designers, and aviation experts are committed to excellence in every aspect of our service.

By 2050, we aim to make urban air mobility accessible to everyone, creating faster connections between communities while reducing the environmental impact of transportation.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to zero-emission transportation and minimizing our environmental footprint at every level.

Safety

Nothing is more important than the safety of our passengers, crew, and the communities we serve.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of technology to improve the passenger experience and operational efficiency.
